Webcomic Roundup Data Deletion

Webcomic Roundup is just a webcomic search and I do not save your personal data. But according to the Facebook rules, we have to provide User Data Deletion Callback URL or Data Deletion Instructions URL. If you want to delete your activities for Webcomic Roundup, you can remove your activities by following the following instructions.

  1. Go to Your Facebook Account's Settings & Privacy. Click ”Settings“.
  2. Then, go to ”Webcomic Roundup” and you will see all of your instant games played activities.
  3. Select the option box of Webcomic Roundup.
